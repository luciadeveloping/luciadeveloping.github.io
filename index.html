My Story

I developed my love for video games at an early age. From Pokémon to Assassin's Creed, I found myself immersed in these vibrant worlds, unable to step away from the screen. Later, during my teenage years, I discovered indie titles and quickly fell in love with the potential of video games to tell stories, evoke powerful emotions, and spark imagination.

Despite my admiration for this medium, my first steps in higher education were more oriented toward audiovisual production. I learned about visual theory and composition, scriptwriting, production management, and more. By the time I earned my Certificate of Higher Education, I had decided to pursue a career in video games. Five years in, I have developed strong technical knowledge as well as valuable skills for the industry.

Why me?

My education, while primarily focused on technical aspects, covered a wide variety of fields related to videogames, such as: 3D modeling, game and sound design, production pipeline, etc. I’ve gained general and specific knowledge that allows me to have a deep understanding of the development process.

During these years, I have worked with a wide variety of languages across diverse projects and acquired skills in programming patterns, concurrent computing, databases and more. I’ve also become familiar with up-to-date technologies and methodologies, including game engines like Phaser and Unity, agile collaboration frameworks such as Scrum, modeling languages like UML, and libraries like Netcode for GameObjects.

Through my experience working with nearly every popular programming language, I have developed a broad understanding of programming as a whole. This has made me highly flexible and able to quickly adapt to and learn new paradigms. I am ready to specialize in related fields such as general programming, UI programming, user experience, game design, quality assurance, etc.

What I’m Currently Working On

As of today, I am in the final year of my degree and currently working on my bachelor’s degree final project. I am developing a demo for a 4X strategy game in Unity. The project focuses on exploring game design strategies to achieve effective storytelling through procedurally generated events.

In addition to my academic work, I am actively seeking internship opportunities at game development studios. I am eager to bring my creativity, technical skills, and passion for gaming to a dynamic team, where I can learn from industry professionals and contribute to the creation of amazing gaming experiences.
